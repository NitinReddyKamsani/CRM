Lead Conversion in Salesforce  : Lead conversion in salesforce is a process in which a lead record is conveerted into
accounts,contacts and opportunities.

i) Lead : lead is a potential customer, individual ror representative from an organization who have shown particular interest 
on your product or service.

ii) Account : represents an organization or cokpany involved in a busniness relationship.

iii) Contact : An individual associated with an account.

iv) Opportunity : A sales deal with a particular account.
